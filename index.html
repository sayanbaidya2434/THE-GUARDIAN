Core Technologies

SHAKE-ALERT TECHNOLOGY (PROXIMITY DEFENSE)

"The Guardian utilizes advanced accelerometer-based algorithms to detect high-velocity displacement of your device. In the event of a sudden snatching or forceful movement, the system instantly triggers an emergency protocol, sending your real-time GPS coordinates to your designated emergency contacts without requiring manual intervention."

STEALTH MODE & FAKE SHUTDOWN SYSTEM

"To prevent unauthorized device deactivation, The Guardian features an industry-first 'Fake Shutdown' mechanism. If an intruder attempts to power off the device, the app simulates a complete system shutdown with a black screen, effectively misleading the intruder while keeping all tracking services and GPS modules active in the background."

DIRECT P2P ENCRYPTED ALERTS

"Safety is decentralized. Our system bypasses third-party servers to ensure that your location data is sent directly from your device to your trusted contacts via encrypted SMS. This ensures zero latency and maximum privacy for your sensitive information."

Permissions & Transparency

SMS PERMISSION (EMERGENCY DISPATCH)

Purpose: To autonomously send SOS messages when a threat is detected.

Trust Factor: The app only accesses SMS functions during a triggered alert. We do not read personal conversations or store your messaging history.

LOCATION PERMISSION (REAL-TIME TRACKING)

Purpose: To provide high-precision Google Maps coordinates to your emergency contacts.

Trust Factor: Location is accessed only when the 'Shake Alert' or 'Fake Shutdown' is active. Your data is never uploaded to any cloud server.

ACCESSIBILITY SERVICE (DEVICE PROTECTION)

Purpose: This is the core of our 'Fake Shutdown' feature. It allows the app to intercept the power-button menu to prevent unauthorized access.

Trust Factor: This permission is used strictly for UI interaction and system-layer protection. We do not monitor your screen activity or capture any keystroke.
